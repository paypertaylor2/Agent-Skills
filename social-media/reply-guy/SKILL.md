# Reply Guy

Smart reply and engagement automation for X.

## When to Use
- Engaging with posts from target accounts
- Building presence in conversations
- Adding value to trending discussions
- Networking with key accounts
- Community engagement

## Reply Styles

See [references/reply-templates.md](references/reply-templates.md) for templates.

### Value-Add Reply
- Adds new information or perspective
- Extends the original point
- Includes data or examples
- Most effective for building authority

### Question Reply
- Asks a thoughtful follow-up question
- Shows genuine curiosity
- Drives conversation forward
- Great for engagement

### Humor Reply
- Witty or funny response
- Relatable to the audience
- Not at anyone's expense
- High viral potential

### Support Reply
- Agrees and amplifies
- Adds personal experience
- Celebrates the original poster
- Builds relationships

### Contrarian Reply
- Respectfully disagrees
- Offers alternative view
- Backed by reasoning
- Sparks healthy debate

## Engagement Rules

See [references/engagement-rules.md](references/engagement-rules.md) for detailed rules.

Key principles:
- Reply within 30 minutes of posting for max visibility
- First 3 replies get the most engagement
- Don't just say "great post" — add value
- Mix reply styles (don't always be the same)
- Engage with accounts in your niche
- Reply to bigger accounts for visibility

## Procedure

1. Identify target post or account
2. Read and understand the content
3. Select appropriate reply style
4. Draft reply (under 280 chars)
5. Review for tone and value
6. Post via xurl
7. Track engagement

## Target Account Strategy

1. List 20-50 accounts in your niche
2. Monitor their posts daily
3. Reply to 3-5 posts per day
4. Rotate targets to avoid pattern detection
5. Build genuine relationships over time

## Rate Limits

- Max 50 replies per hour
- Max 200 replies per day
- Space out replies (min 30 seconds between)
- Don't reply to the same account more than 3x/day

## Safety

- Never spam
- Don't use identical replies
- Avoid controversial takes on sensitive topics
- Don't engage with obvious bots
- Follow X automation rules
