# Engagement Rules

## Timing
- Reply within 30 minutes for max visibility
- First 3 replies get the most engagement
- Peak hours: 9-11 AM, 1-3 PM, 5-7 PM EST
- Avoid late night (12-6 AM) unless targeting global audience

## Frequency
- Max 50 replies per hour
- Max 200 replies per day
- Space replies 30+ seconds apart
- Don't reply to same account more than 3x/day

## Target Selection
- Focus on accounts in your niche
- Prioritize accounts with 10K-500K followers (engagement sweet spot)
- Engage with bigger accounts for visibility
- Build relationships with peers (similar follower count)

## Content Rules
- Always add value (never just "great post")
- Match the tone of the original post
- Keep replies under 200 characters
- Use questions to drive conversation
- Include personal experience when relevant

## What NOT to Do
- Don't spam identical replies
- Don't argue aggressively
- Don't self-promote in replies
- Don't engage with obvious bots
- Don't reply to controversial takes unless prepared
- Don't use reply sections as your blog

## Relationship Building
- Reply consistently to the same accounts
- Remember previous conversations
- Support others' content genuinely
- Celebrate others' wins
- Be authentic, not transactional

## Tracking
- Log which replies get the most engagement
- Note which accounts engage back
- Track follower growth from engagement
- Adjust strategy based on results
