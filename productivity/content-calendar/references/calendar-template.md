```

9:00 AM — Daily digest thread
2:00 PM — Educational thread: [topic]
7:00 PM — Engagement round (reply to 5 posts)

TUESDAY:
9:00 AM — Daily digest
12:00 PM — Meme post
5:00 PM — Quote tweet with commentary

WEDNESDAY:
9:00 AM — Daily digest
2:00 PM — Thread: "[hot take]"
7:00 PM — Engagement round

THURSDAY:
9:00 AM — Daily digest
12:00 PM — Token analysis post
5:00 PM — Reply to mentions

FRIDAY:
9:00 AM — Daily digest
2:00 PM — Week recap thread
7:00 PM — Community engagement

SATURDAY:
12:00 PM — Casual/meme post

SUNDAY:
Rest day or:
12:00 PM — Week ahead preview
```

## Content Pillars

Define 3-5 content pillars for consistency:

1. **Educational** — Teach something valuable
2. **Entertainment** — Memes, humor, relatable content
3. **Engagement** — Questions, polls, discussions
4. **Analysis** — Token research, market takes
5. **Community** — Celebrate others, build relationships

## Posting Frequency

| Content Type | Frequency | Best Time |
|--------------|-----------|-----------|
| Daily digest | 1x/day | 9 AM |
| Educational thread | 2-3x/week | 2 PM |
| Meme | 1-2x/week | 12 PM |
| Quote tweet | 1x/day | 5 PM |
| Engagement | 2x/day | 9 AM / 7 PM |
| Token analysis | 1-2x/week | 2 PM |
| Week recap | 1x/week | Friday 2 PM |
